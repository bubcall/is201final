Above is a chart on the songs played in the popular tv show "Stranger Things" and their popularity as well as which era they came from. here is a list of my 3 favorite songs from the show and why.

  • #1: Master of Puppets by Metallica
    • The heavy rock fits the scene perfectly in the show
    • One of the greatest guitar solos of all time is in this song
    • Heavy drums, heavy guitar, heavy metal.
    • Metallica embodies an 80's punk perfectly
  • #2: Running Up That Hill by Kate Bush
    • This song encapsulates the feelings Max has in the show
    • the theme of this song is somber yet hopeful
    • the progressive build to the end of the song pushes tension
    • It made a huge comeback after this episode released, it's cool to see old songs coming forth again.
  • #3: You Don't Mess Around With Jim by Jim Croce
    • It has a playful theme that carries comedy in the show
    • It helps us dive into Jim Hopper as a character
    • It's bounciness can get anyone up and moving on their feet
